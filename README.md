Example:

Straightforward Prompts:

• "Define photosynthesis in one sentence."

Tabular Format Prompting:

• "Compare and contrast AC and DC current in a table."

Preceding Question Prompting:

• "Why is climate change a global concern? Explain how greenhouse gases contribute to global warming."

Missing Word Prompting:

• "The capital of France is ____."

• "In photosynthesis, plants absorb sunlight to produce ____."

OUTPUT:

1. Straightforward Prompts
Straightforward prompts are direct instructions asking for a specific output without extra structure.

Scenario A: Designing a zero-waste urban garden
"Design a zero-waste urban balcony garden for a small apartment in Chennai that uses recycled materials, supports local pollinators, and yields herbs year-round. Include a list of 5 plants, materials needed, and a simple setup guide."

Scenario B: Planning a community drone light show
"Plan a drone light show for a local festival in rural Tamil Nadu using 50 affordable drones. Detail the theme inspired by Pongal harvest, flight patterns, safety measures, and estimated budget under ₹50,000."

2. Tabular Format Prompting
These prompts require output in a table for organized comparison or listing.

Scenario A: Comparing electric scooter batteries
"Compare three electric scooter battery types suitable for Chennai's humid climate: lithium-ion, solid-state, and sodium-ion. Use a table with columns for capacity (Wh), charge time (hours), lifespan (cycles), cost (₹), and heat resistance rating."

Scenario B: Evaluating rooftop solar panel suppliers
"Evaluate four rooftop solar panel suppliers in Tamil Nadu based on 1kW system costs. Create a table with rows for suppliers (Tata, Adani, Vikram, Local Co-op) and columns for price (₹/kW), warranty (years), efficiency (%), installation time (days), and customer rating (out of 5)."

3. Preceding Question Prompting
These start with a guiding question to set context, followed by the main task.

Scenario A: What makes a podcast episode on ancient Tamil maritime trade engaging?
"Building on that, script a 10-minute podcast episode outline for 'Tamil Seas: Forgotten Voyages,' including intro hook, 3 key historical facts with sources, guest interview questions, and a modern relevance tie-in to Chennai's ports."

Scenario B: How do bioluminescent fungi thrive in Tamil Nadu's Western Ghats?
"Using that insight, propose a citizen science app feature for tracking these fungi. Detail user interface screens, data collection methods (photos, GPS, notes), gamification elements, and partnership ideas with local forest departments."

4. Missing Word Prompting
These provide a prompt with blanks for the AI to fill, encouraging creative or precise completions.

Scenario A: Recipe for a fusion ______ cuisine dish
"Complete this recipe for a fusion [Tamil Nadu street food meets Japanese ramen] dish called 'Idli Ramen.' Fill in the missing words: Ingredients: 12 steamed _____, 200g ______ broth, 100g ______ toppings, ______ sauce. Steps: 1. Simmer broth with [spice]. 2. Assemble in bowl with [arrangement]. Serves 4."

Scenario B: Blueprint for a ______ powered village microgrid
"Fill in the blanks for a blueprint of a [coconut husk biochar] powered village microgrid in coastal Tamil Nadu: Components: ______ generator (produces 10kW), ______ storage banks (5kWh each), ______ inverters. Installation: Dig ______ trenches, connect to 20 homes. Output: Powers ______ daily from dusk to dawn."
